The Perfect Brew

A simple ritual to unlock the full flavor and health benefits of your herbal blend.

  1. 1

    Boil Water

    Bring fresh water to a rolling boil (212°F / 100°C).

  2. 2

    Measure

    Add 1 heaping spoon of Hibiscus blend per cup.

  3. 3

    Steep

    Let it steep for 5–7 minutes for optimal extraction.

  4. 4

    Strain

    Remove the herbs or tea bag from your cup.

  5. 5

    Enjoy

    Serve hot, or pour over ice for a refreshing cooler.
